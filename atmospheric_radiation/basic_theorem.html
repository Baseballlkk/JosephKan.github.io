Solid Angle

definition:$$\Omega=\frac{\sigma}{r^2}$$

for infinitesimal solid angle:

$$d\Omega=\frac{d\sigma}{r^2}$$

for infinitesimal surface area:

$$d\sigma=(r\cdot d\theta)(rsin\theta d\phi)$$

$$\Rightarrow d\Omega=sin\theta d\theta d\phi$$

Physical quatities of radiation

Radiation Intensity

for energy at a specific wavelength:

$$dE_\lambda=I_\lambda \cdot cos\theta dA \cdot d\lambda \cdot dt \cdot d\Omega$$
